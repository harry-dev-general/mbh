Emergency Procedures

Person Overboard

  • Shout "MAN OVERBOARD"
  • Throw flotation device
  • Keep visual contact
  • Execute safe recovery
  • Call Water Police if needed

Fire on Board

  • Alert all passengers
  • Use fire extinguisher/blanket
  • Radio MAYDAY if spreading
  • Prepare for evacuation
  • Head for nearest shore

Medical Emergency

  • Radio for ambulance
  • Provide first aid
  • Head to nearest access point
  • Keep patient comfortable
  • Document incident

Weather Limits

Wind Speed Limits

  • Under 20kts: Safe to operate
  • 20kts S/SE: Monitor closely
  • Over 20kts: Cancel/Recall all vessels

Safety Equipment

  • Life jackets (correct sizes)

  • First aid kit
  • Anchor and rode
  • Bilge pump
  • Navigation lights
  • Sound signals

Engine Checks

  • Oil levels
  • Tell tail (water outlet)
  • Start/stop test
  • Steering operation
  • Throttle and gears

Quick Troubleshooting

Engine Won't Start

  • Check battery connections
  • Fuel valve open?
  • Kill switch position
  • In neutral?
  • Try pull cord

Overheating

  • Check water intake
  • Reduce speed
  • Return to base

Call for Help When

  • Any safety concern
  • Major mechanical failure
  • Medical emergency
  • Weather deterioration
